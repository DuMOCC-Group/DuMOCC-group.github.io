# Opportunities

## Postdoctoral fellowships

- [Career Development Fellowship](https://royalsociety.org/grants/career-development-fellowship/) for researchers from a Black heritage, including mixed Black background.
- [Daphne Jackson Fellowship](https://daphnejackson.org/) for those returning after a career break.
- [EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/) to support international mobility.
- [Levelhulme Trust Early Career Fellowship](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/early-career-fellowships) for researchers who have not yet held a full-time permanent academic post.
- [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Fellowship](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships) for researchers moving within Europe or coming to Europe.
- [Newton International Fellowship](https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/) for non-UK scientists who wish to conduct research in the UK.
- [Royal Academy of Engineering](https://raeng.org.uk/programmes-and-prizes/programmes/uk-grants-and-prizes) sometimes offers targeted postdoctoral fellowships.
- [Royal Commission for the Exhibition of 1851 Fellowship](https://royalcommission1851.org/) for early career STEM researchers.
